Our Methodology

NutriShop isn't just a meal planner—it's a mathematical optimization engine designed to solve the complex problem of meeting 100% of your nutritional needs at the lowest possible cost.

Core Pillars

🥗 Complete Nutrition

We strictly adhere to RDA and UL standards to ensure no deficiencies and no toxicity.

💷 Cost Optimization

Our algorithms compare prices across UK stores to find the most cost-effective path to health.

🛒 Real-World Availability

We map results to actual products in your local supermarkets, ensuring every list is actionable.

🍳 Equipment Match

We optimize meals for your facilities—whether you have a full range or just a microwave.

Nutritional Standards

We use data from the USDA FoodData Central and UK CoFID databases. Our constraints are based on peer-reviewed standards:

  • RDA (Recommended Dietary Allowance): Standards for 98% of healthy people.
  • UL (Tolerable Upper Intake Level): Maximum daily intake to avoid toxicity.
  • AMDR (Acceptable Macronutrient Distribution Range): Balanced energy source ratios.

Health Constraints & Safety

Beyond basic vitamins and minerals, we apply specific constraints based on modern health research to reduce long-term disease risks.

🥩 Red & Processed Meat Limits

Why? The World Health Organization (WHO) classifies processed meat as carcinogenic (Group 1) and red meat as probably carcinogenic (Group 2A). High intake is linked to colorectal cancer.

Our Limit: We cap red meat intake to <350g per week and minimize processed meats, favoring poultry, fish, and plant-based proteins.

🧂 Sodium & Sugar

Sodium: Capped at 2,300mg/day to reduce blood pressure and cardiovascular risk.
Added Sugar: Limited to <10% of total calories to prevent metabolic syndrome and obesity.

🥬 Fiber & Gut Health

We enforce a minimum fiber intake (30g/day) to support microbiome health, digestion, and satiety.
